 
# **Privacy Policy for Photo App**

**Last Updated: 2025-01-01**

Thank you for using **Photo – AI Product Photography App** (“Photo”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Photo mobile application.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

### **1.1. Photos & Media**

* The app requires access to your camera and photos for the purpose of capturing and editing product images.
* Photos taken or uploaded through the app remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.
* We do **not** automatically upload or store your photos on our servers.

### **1.2. Device Information**

We may collect anonymized device information such as:

* Device model
* Operating system version
* App version
* Performance logs (to improve stability)

This information is used for diagnostics and does not identify you personally.

### **1.3. Usage Data**

To improve the app, we may use analytics tools that collect:

* Feature usage
* Crash reports
* Interaction events

This data is anonymous and does not include personal information.

---

## **2. How We Use Your Information**

We use collected information to:

* Provide the core functionality of the app (AI processing, editing tools)
* Improve app performance and user experience
* Fix bugs and optimize performance
* Analyze app usage patterns to enhance features

We **do not** sell or share personal data with third parties.

---

## **3. AI Processing**

Some features may use on-device AI or secure cloud-based AI processing.
When cloud processing is used:

* Images are processed temporarily
* They are not stored permanently
* They are automatically deleted after processing

We ensure that data is encrypted during transfer.

---

## **4. Permissions**

The app may request the following permissions:

* **Camera Access:** To capture product images.
* **Photos/Gallery Access:** To import or save photos.
* **Internet Access:** Only when using AI-based features or downloading templates/backgrounds.

Permissions are used strictly for their intended purposes.

---

## **5. Data Security**

We take reasonable and industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## **6. Third-Party Services**

We may use trusted third-party services for:

* Analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics)
* Crash reporting (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics)
* AI processing (secure cloud-based services)

These services follow their own privacy policies and comply with GDPR/CCPA where applicable.

---

## **7. Children’s Privacy**

The app is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## **8. Your Rights**

Depending on your region, you may have rights regarding:

* Access to your data
* Correcting inaccurate data
* Requesting data deletion

Contact us to submit a request.

---

## **9. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this policy from time to time.
Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
